About Our Company

In 2018 we wanted to open up a cafe where people could come to relax, and enjoy a wide variety of food and drinks. Sometimes we felt live giving up, and then we came across another thing to tell us not to. At the end of 2019 we finally opened up, until the pandemic hit, business was great. The pandemic nearly wiped out our entire business.
